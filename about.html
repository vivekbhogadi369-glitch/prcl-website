About Prateek Road Carriers

Hyderabad-based Prateek Road Carriers Private Limited is committed to delivering dependable road transport and logistics solutions across India through professionalism, reliability and long-term business relationships.

Who We Are

Prateek Road Carriers Private Limited is a Hyderabad-based road transport and logistics company providing dependable Full Truck Load (FTL), Part Load (PTL), industrial freight and Pan India transportation solutions. With an annual turnover exceeding ₹15 crore, PRCL has earned the trust of manufacturers, distributors, traders and businesses through reliable coordination, timely deliveries, professional service and a strong customer-first approach.
